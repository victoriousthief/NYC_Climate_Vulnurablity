About the Heat Vulnerability Index (HVI)

The Heat Vulnerability Index (HVI) identifies neighborhoods at higher risk during extreme heat events based on factors such as age, income, health, housing, and environmental conditions. It helps planners and communities prioritize cooling strategies, green infrastructure, and outreach where they are most needed.
